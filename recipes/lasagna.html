Lasagna

Description

A decadent dish the whole family will enjoy. Well, unless they don't like ricotta cheese, like my wife.

Ingredients

  • noodles
  • cheese
  • meat
  • Ragu

Steps

  1. Cook Your Meat
  2. Add Ragu to meat
  3. Cook your noodles?
  4. Make a noodle layer
  5. Make a Meat layer
  6. Make a Cheese Layer
  7. Repeat until satisfied
  8. Preheat oven and bake, just use your gut for the temperature and time
